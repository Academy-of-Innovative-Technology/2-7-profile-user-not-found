About The Goat

Aubrey Drake Graham, known as Drake, is a Canadian rapper, singer, and actor who rose to fame on Degrassi: The Next Generation before becoming one of the best-selling and most-awarded music artists globally, known for blending singing and rapping, achieving numerous Billboard records, and influencing modern hip-hop with his introspective yet vulnerable style. Born in Toronto on October 24, 1986, he's a dual citizen (Canada/US) with a successful career spanning acting, music, and entrepreneurship, often called "Champagne Papi" or "Drizzy".

Drake is known for his record-breaking music career as a rapper and singer, blending hip-hop with R&B, his early acting role on Degrassi, and becoming a dominant force in pop culture through viral hits, sentimental lyrics, and business ventures, often called the "Artist of the Decade" for the 2010s. He's famous for chart dominance, numerous #1 hits, record streaming numbers, and pioneering a sensitive, melodic rap style that influenced many artists.

#Education

Education

He dropped out of high school to pursue acting on Degrassi, but later returned to complete his diploma through correspondence in 2012, describing it as one of the best feelings of his life. He attended Forest Hill Collegiate Institute and Vaughan Road Academy in Toronto.

Career Start

Experience
